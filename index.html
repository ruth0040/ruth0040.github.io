Logic Meets Creative

Hey-o! Coding, coding, coding. Many designers cringe at the logical and very technical side to making a website or application. Not me. Off the start of every project I bounce in my seat, excited to be telling the computer how to act. Since being a child I have always been a very diverse person in my likes, polar opposites actually. Being at home in math class as well as in art, weird right?

Not me. I thrive in environments where I can solve problems in creative and logical ways. Graphic Design (to me) is that happy medium, combining the left and the right brain at the same time and engaging them both equally. This is why I am a designer.
